Hospitality and Institutional Management Department

Crafting Excellence in Hospitality and Culinary Arts

Department Overview

The Hospitality and Institutional Management Department at Ruiru Technical and Vocational College is dedicated to nurturing the next generation of hospitality professionals. Our programs are designed to provide students with a perfect blend of theoretical knowledge and hands-on experience in the dynamic fields of hospitality, culinary arts, and institutional management.

With state-of-the-art training kitchens, a student-run restaurant, and strong industry partnerships, we offer a range of courses from foundational culinary skills to advanced hospitality management. Our curriculum is regularly updated to reflect the latest industry trends, sustainable practices, and technological advancements in the hospitality sector.

Message from the Head of Department

"Our department is committed to cultivating excellence in hospitality education. We focus on developing not just technical skills, but also the soft skills crucial in this people-oriented industry. Our goal is to produce graduates who are not only proficient in their craft but also possess the leadership, communication, and problem-solving skills needed to thrive in the global hospitality sector."

- Chef Michael Ochieng, Head of Hospitality and Institutional Management Department
