Discover RTVC

Ruiru Technical and Vocational College is a Government sponsored TVET institution located in the industrial town of Ruiru. We offer quality Competency Based Education and Training (CBET) with state-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty.

  • Quality Competency Based Education and Training (CBET)
  • Professional career guidance
  • Vibrant student experience
  • Our programs are demand driven, responsive and adaptive
  • Timely completion and Competency
  • Serene learning environment

Why Choose RTVC?

Quality Education

Our programs are designed to meet industry standards and provide students with relevant, up-to-date skills.

Experienced Faculty

Learn from industry professionals and experienced educators who are passionate about your success.

Career Opportunities

Practical Training
