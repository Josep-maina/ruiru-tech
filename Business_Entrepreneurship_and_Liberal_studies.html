Business, Entrepreneurship and Liberal Studies Department

Empowering Future Leaders and Innovators

Department Overview

The Business, Entrepreneurship and Liberal Studies Department at Ruiru Technical and Vocational College is dedicated to developing well-rounded professionals equipped with business acumen, entrepreneurial spirit, and a broad understanding of social sciences. Our programs prepare students for diverse career paths in business, social work, and beyond.

Message from the Head of Department

"Our department is committed to nurturing future leaders, entrepreneurs, and social change-makers. We provide a comprehensive education that combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills, preparing our students to make meaningful contributions in various sectors of society."

- Dr. Sarah Johnson, Head of Business, Entrepreneurship and Liberal Studies Department
