About Ruiru Technical and Vocational College

Discover the Ruiru TVC Difference

Ruiru Technical and Vocational College is a TVET CDACC (TVET Curriculum Development, Assessment and Certification Council) accredited institution. We are located in Kiambu county, Ruiru Constituency, Gatongora ward, 2Km off Eastern Bypass (kamakis), 1Km off Karuguru Kwihota stage or 5km from Rainbow - St. Peter Catholic Church Route.

The college has state-of-the-art training equipment and is currently accredited to offer CDACC Level 4-6 training in various skill areas. Ruiru TVC upholds CBET's hands-on skills-acquisition approach.

We also serve as an ICT hub in Ruiru Constituency, Gatongora Ward, with a modern 100-seater capacity Jitume ICT Lab for online jobs training for the community at large.

The college also nurtures talents in creative arts (Drama & Music), Football, Handball, volleyball, badminton, athletics and indoor games.

Our History

Ruiru Technical and Vocational College (RTVC) has a brief but impactful history.

The college was established to meet the growing demand for technical education in the Ruiru area. It opened its doors in 2023, built on a generous 7-acre piece of land donated by the local community. The construction was funded by the National Government Constituencies Development Fund (NG-CDF), which allocated Ksh 10 million for this project.

Initially, RTVC was managed and mentored by Kabete National Polytechnic, which helped set up the institution and ensure it met educational standards. The college was designed to accommodate around 1,000 students, offering various technical and vocational courses, with the aim of equipping youths with knowledge, practical skills and market driven competencies for self-reliance: "Ujuzi wa kujitegemea."

In 2024, a significant milestone was reached when the college received its own local management board. This change allowed RTVC to operate more independently and focus on tailoring its programs to better serve the needs of the community and its students.

Today, Ruiru Technical and Vocational College continues to play a vital role in empowering youths through education and skill development, contributing to the local economy and workforce.
