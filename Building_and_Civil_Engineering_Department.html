Building and Civil Engineering Department

Shaping the Future of Construction and Infrastructure

Department Overview

The Building and Civil Engineering Department at Ruiru Technical and Vocational College is committed to providing high-quality education in the fields of construction, structural engineering, and infrastructure development. Our programs are designed to equip students with practical skills and theoretical knowledge essential for success in the dynamic construction industry.

With state-of-the-art workshops, experienced trainers, and strong industry partnerships, we offer a range of courses from foundational building technology to advanced civil engineering. Our curriculum is regularly updated to reflect the latest industry standards, sustainable practices, and technological advancements in the field.

Message from the Head of Department

"Our department is dedicated to nurturing the next generation of builders, civil engineers, and construction managers. We focus on hands-on learning, innovative design techniques, and sustainable construction practices. Our goal is to produce graduates who are not only technically proficient but also environmentally conscious and ready to tackle the complex challenges of modern infrastructure development."

- Eng. Jane Smith, Head of Building and Civil Engineering Department
