Fashion Design and Cosmetology Department

Unleashing Creativity in Fashion and Beauty

Department Overview

The Fashion Design and Cosmetology Department at Ruiru Technical and Vocational College is dedicated to nurturing creative talents in the dynamic worlds of fashion and beauty. Our programs are designed to provide students with a perfect blend of artistic vision and technical skills needed to thrive in these ever-evolving industries.

With state-of-the-art design studios, beauty labs, and strong industry partnerships, we offer a range of courses from foundational fashion design to advanced cosmetology techniques. Our curriculum is regularly updated to reflect the latest trends, sustainable practices, and technological advancements in the fashion and beauty sectors.

Message from the Head of Department

"Our department is committed to fostering creativity, innovation, and professionalism in the fields of fashion design and cosmetology. We focus on developing not just technical skills, but also the artistic vision and entrepreneurial spirit crucial in these creative industries. Our goal is to produce graduates who are not only skilled craftspeople but also trendsetters and innovators in the global fashion and beauty landscape."

- Ms. Grace Muthoni, Head of Fashion Design and Cosmetology Department
